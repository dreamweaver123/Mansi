City Guide

Eat Well

Are you ready to discover healthy and delicious local flavor? Whether you're called to Japanese, Vegetarian, Italian, or French fare, or simply like to relax at your neighborhood cafe, you can easily eat well and feel great in Meridien. With so many options, your body will be fueled and your palate satisfied.

Enjoy Everything

If you're looking to enjoy your surroundings to the fullest, you've come to the right place. Meridien offers the activity of a big city while taking pride in a small town sense of conscience and community. Here, you can experience everything, from modern city nightlife to small co-op gardens.

Move Here

Move about the city and take in the beautiful landscape, whether you ride a bike, walk, or utilize our efficient transportation system. In Meridien, it's easy to get to and from work, meet up with friends, and explore the city without ever putting a key in the ignition.

Live Fully

City life doesn't have to be a one-way street. Meridiennes know how to utilize their resources while giving back to the environment. Here, you can live in harmony with your setting, and discover the balance and well-being the city has to offer.
